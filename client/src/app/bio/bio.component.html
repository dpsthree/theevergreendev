About

Paul Spears

The Evergreen Dev — consultative technologist and entrepreneur, partnering with organizations to design, build, and launch technology with principle.

Paul Spears is Head of Development at Open Kingdom Development, the software practice within Kingdom Solutions. He leads a team that partners with businesses to design and build technology, with a focus on launching and strengthening companies in ways that are both effective and principled.

Previously, Paul served as Head of AI at Valiantys, where he led AI strategy and product innovation, including the development of the Rovo AI Jump-Start framework. He also held the role of Global Head of R&D and Solution Architecture, guiding distributed teams on AI-driven platforms and enterprise transformation initiatives. Earlier in his career at Oasis Digital, he led Fortune 500 delivery efforts, contributed to large-scale platform development, and helped build and scale the Angular Boot Camp program.

Paul is also a contributing architect at Hope Technologies, where he works on WonderWall—a digital care platform designed to support dementia patients and their caregivers through personalized media, circadian lighting, scheduling, and integrated cloud and local systems. This work reflects his belief that well-designed technology should serve as a quiet, reliable partner — especially for those who rely on others for care.

He has spoken at international conferences, meetups, and podcasts; built developer communities with thousands of members; and collaborated with organizations like Google on initiatives ranging from framework alignment to enterprise platform standardization and secure IoT onboarding.

Paul is passionate about helping teams operate at higher levels of abstraction, mentoring engineers, and connecting open-source practices with enterprise strategy.

Outside of his professional work, Paul leads his family with a clear and shared mission: to love God wholeheartedly, to follow where He leads, and to grow together in Scripture, prayer, generosity, and service. Their aim is to live faithfully, steward what they’ve been given, and give glory to God in both their work and their lives.
